Study Abroad Guidance for International Students, Online and In Person
BHE UNI provides study abroad guidance for international students applying to the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Ireland, China, and New Zealand. Get admissions support, student visa readiness guidance, scholarship guidance, and accommodation planning online worldwide or in person at one of our offices. Start with a free consultation to confirm eligibility and set a clear application timeline.

Why students choose BHE UNI
Experienced study abroad guidance from dedicated counsellors
With 14+ years of experience, BHE UNI supports international students through key stages of the study abroad journey. Our dedicated counsellors help you stay organised, follow a realistic application timeline, and keep your documents consistent across your university application and student visa readiness steps.
Support for your study destinations
We work with applicants targeting the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Ireland, China, and New Zealand, using clear requirements checks and practical planning so you always know the next action.
Trackable journey with our platform
You can track progress in our application tracking portal and mobile app, including deadlines, status updates, and what to prepare next.
Best fit course and university shortlisting
Using AI course matching plus adviser review, we build a realistic shortlist based on your goals, eligibility, budget, and preferred location.
Scholarship aware planning
We provide scholarship guidance with typical timelines and document expectations, so you can prepare early and avoid missed deadlines.
Structured visa readiness guidance
For routes that require it, we provide student visa guidance with a visa document checklist, biometrics preparation guidance where applicable, and CAS guidance for UK routes where applicable. Visa decisions are made by relevant authorities and outcomes vary by case.

Our services for international students
University admissions support
We provide university admissions support including:
course and university selection and a realistic university shortlist
entry requirement checks and planning
SOP guidance and personal statement guidance where relevant
references, CV, and document planning using a document checklist
Student visa guidance and readiness
Where a visa is required, we provide student visa guidance and visa readiness guidance focused on preparation and timelines, including:
visa document checklist
biometrics preparation guidance where applicable
CAS guidance for UK routes where applicable
Visa decisions are made by relevant authorities and outcomes vary by case.
Scholarship guidance
We provide scholarship guidance on eligibility, deadlines, and common supporting documents.
Accommodation guidance
We provide student accommodation advice on:
halls vs private accommodation
booking timelines, deposits, and tenancy basics
For UK accommodation planning, independent guidance is available from UKCISA. (ukcisa.org.uk)
Post arrival support
Our post arrival support includes settling in support such as arrival planning, orientation steps, and basic student budget planning guidance.

FAQs
Which study abroad destinations do you support?
We support applications to the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Ireland, China, and New Zealand.
Is the free consultation really free?
Yes. Your first free consultation covers eligibility, shortlisting, and next steps.
Can you help me choose the right course and university?
Yes. We combine AI course matching with adviser review to create a realistic university shortlist.
Do you provide student visa guidance?
Yes. We provide student visa guidance and visa readiness guidance for routes that require it, including visa document checklist support.

Yes. We provide accommodation guidance including halls vs private accommodation planning.
