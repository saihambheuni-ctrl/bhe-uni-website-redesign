Common Visa Issues We Help You Avoid

Preparation starts too late

Late planning forces rushed evidence collection.

Our visa timeline keeps tasks realistic against deadlines and intake dates.

Incomplete or inconsistent documents

Missing pages, unclear scans, or mismatched details slow progress.

UK student visa support

Applicants must provide a passport and a CAS, and may need evidence such as proof of funds, ATAS, or TB test results depending on their circumstances. We support CAS guidance, visa timeline planning, and a tailored document checklist.

Source: UK Student visa documents (gov.uk)

Canada study permit guidance

IRCC provides a study permit document checklist and expectations such as IMM 5483. We support evidence planning and readiness checks aligned to those expectations.

Source: IRCC checklist (canada.ca)

Australia student visa guidance

Australia provides an official document checklist tool, and international students must hold OSHC for the duration of study. We support checklist-led preparation and timeline mapping.

Source: immi.homeaffairs.gov.au · studyaustralia.gov.au

No Student Service Fee

BHE UNI does not charge students for its visa support services. Your consultation, visa guidance, document checklist support, and adviser-led preparation are provided free of charge. We explain the process clearly so you know what to expect before you proceed.
