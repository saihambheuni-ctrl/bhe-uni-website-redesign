About Dr Mohammad Shafiq

Dr Mohammad Shafiq's leadership profile reflects a strong belief in education as a route to knowledge, personal development, and professional growth. At BHE UNI, this leadership perspective is connected to a wider mission of helping students make informed decisions about their academic future and access suitable higher education pathways.

BHE UNI has been helping students for more than a decade to identify the right path for their higher education journey. The organisation also helps reduce the gap between prospective students and higher education institutions through practical support, strong relationships, and experience. As Director, Dr Mohammad Shafiq represents the leadership behind that student-focused approach.
